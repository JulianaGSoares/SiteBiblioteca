A library is a place where a collection of books, magazines, newspapers, and other resources are kept for public use or study. It provides access to information and knowledge in various forms, both physical and digital. Libraries offer a quiet space for reading, research, and learning, and often provide services like borrowing books, internet access, and educational programs. They play an essential role in promoting literacy, learning, and community engagement.
